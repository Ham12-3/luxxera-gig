# Logo Specifications for Patient Confidence Hub

## Ideal Logo Description

### Overall Concept
A modern, professional logo combining a shield icon with the text "Patient Confidence Hub" in a horizontal layout. The logo should convey trust, safety, and medical excellence while maintaining a clean, approachable aesthetic.

---

## Detailed Design Description

### 1. Icon/Mark (Left Side)

**Shape: Shield**
- Modern, rounded shield shape (not sharp or medieval)
- Soft, rounded corners matching the app's design language
- Width to height ratio: approximately 1:1.2 (slightly taller than wide)
- Overall size: Should be proportional to text (see sizing below)

**Elements Inside Shield:**
- **Checkmark**: Bold, white checkmark (âœ“) inside the shield
- Checkmark should be prominent and clearly visible
- Position: Centered within the shield
- Style: Clean, geometric checkmark (not handwritten)

**Colors:**
- Shield outline/stroke: Primary green (`#4CAF50` or `hsl(142, 76%, 36%)`)
- Shield fill: Transparent or very subtle green tint (optional)
- Checkmark: White (`#FFFFFF`)
- Optional: Subtle inner glow or highlight for depth

**Style:**
- Flat design with minimal depth (avoid heavy gradients or 3D effects)
- Clean, bold lines (2-3px stroke weight for shield outline)
- Modern and minimalist
- Should work in both light and dark contexts

---

### 2. Typography (Right Side)

**Text:** "Patient Confidence Hub"

**Font:**
- **Family**: Inter (matching the application font)
- **Weight**: Semi-bold (600) or Bold (700)
- **Style**: Regular (not italic)

**Typography Details:**
- Letter spacing: Slightly increased (0.5-1px tracking) for premium feel
- Color: White or very light gray (`#FFFFFF` or `#F5F5F5`)
- Case: Title Case (each word capitalized)
- Line height: Normal to slightly tight (1.0-1.1)

**Layout:**
- Single line (no line breaks)
- Aligned to the left edge of the text
- Vertical alignment: Centered with the shield icon

---

### 3. Overall Layout

**Composition:**
- Horizontal layout (icon left, text right)
- Gap between icon and text: 12-16px
- Vertical alignment: Both icon and text centered on the same baseline
- Total logo width: Approximately 4:1 ratio (4 parts width to 1 part height)

**Spacing:**
- Clear space around logo: Minimum equal to the height of the shield icon
- Padding: Comfortable spacing to prevent cramped appearance

---

## Size Specifications

### Primary Logo (Header Use)

**Dimensions:**
- **Height**: 40-48px (for header display)
- **Width**: Auto (maintains aspect ratio, typically 200-250px wide)
- **Format**: PNG with transparency or SVG

**File Specifications:**
- **SVG** (recommended): Vector format, scalable, crisp at any size
- **PNG**: 
  - High resolution: 800x160px (2x for retina)
  - Standard: 400x80px
  - With transparency

**Usage Context:**
- Header navigation bar
- Height: 40px (h-10 in Tailwind)
- Should be readable and clear at this size

---

### Logo Variations Needed

#### 1. Full Logo (Icon + Text)
- **Size**: 400x80px (standard), 800x160px (retina)
- **Use**: Header, marketing materials, landing pages
- **Format**: SVG (preferred) or PNG with transparency

#### 2. Icon Only (Shield with Checkmark)
- **Size**: 64x64px, 128x128px, 256x256px, 512x512px
- **Use**: Favicon, app icon, social media profile, small spaces
- **Format**: SVG or PNG (square format)
- **Important**: Must be recognizable without text

#### 3. Text Only (Wordmark)
- **Size**: Variable width, 40-48px height
- **Use**: Footer, email signatures, when space is limited
- **Format**: SVG or PNG

## Technical Requirements

### File Formats

**Primary:**
- **SVG**: Vector format, scalable, recommended for web
- **PNG**: Raster format with transparency, for specific use cases

**Sizes to Provide:**
- Full logo: 400x80px, 800x160px (2x for retina)
- Icon only: 32x32px, 64x64px, 128x128px, 256x256px, 512x512px
- Favicon: 32x32px, 64x64px (ICO format)

### Quality Standards

- **Resolution**: Minimum 2x for retina displays
- **Transparency**: PNG with alpha channel
- **Optimization**: Compressed but high quality
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (SVG preferred for scalability)

## Design Principles

### Simplicity
- Clean, uncluttered design
- Works well at small sizes (favicon: 32x32px)
- Recognizable even when scaled down
- No unnecessary decorative elements

### Professionalism
- Medical/healthcare appropriate
- Trustworthy and reliable appearance
- Modern but timeless (not trendy)
- Suitable for international medical tourism context

### Versatility
- Works in color and monochrome
- Adapts to different contexts (header, footer, favicon)
- Maintains clarity at various sizes
- Readable on both light and dark backgrounds

### Brand Alignment
- Matches the dark green theme (`#4CAF50`)
- Complements the app's clean, modern aesthetic
- Suggests safety, trust, and confidence
- Professional medical/healthcare feel

- Shield height: 40-48px
